About

I am a 3rd year design student at the School of Interactive Art and Technology at SFU with an intense curiosity and passion for experience and analysis. As humans, experience is everything– it is our senses that allow us to find meaning in the world around us and understanding within ourselves. Even the smallest interactions we have can have lasting and meaningful impacts on us. As a designer, I hope to create beautiful interfaces and experiences that people can find pleasure in, down to the smallest details, because it’s the little things that count.
